FAQ - How To Use This Website

1) How do I log in?

Use your apartment account (for example bloc1_apt1) and your assigned password. Usernames are lowercase.

2) How do I share my parking spot?

In Share My Parking Spot, enter your parking spot number, choose underground/above ground, then set exact start and end date/time and press Share Spot.

3) How do I claim an available spot?

In Claim Shared Parking Spot, filter by building/type (or keep Any), select a free spot, set the period you need, add contact phone, then press Claim Selected Spot.

4) What happens if I claim only part of the shared period?

The claimed interval is reserved for you, and the remaining time before/after your claim stays available for other neighbours.

5) Where do I see free spots and claimed history?

Use dashboard tables: Shared Parking Spots (Free), My Shared Spots, My Shared Spots Claimed By Neighbours, and My Claimed Spots.

6) What can the admin user do?

Admin can create users, create shared slots for residents, reset default users, and monitor all users and parking activity.
