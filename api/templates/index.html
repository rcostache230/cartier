How To Use This Website

1) How do I log in?

Use your apartment account (for example bloc1_apt1) and your assigned password. Usernames are lowercase.

1.1) What timezone is used?

All dates and times across parking and voting are shown and managed in Bucharest time.

2) How do I share my parking spot?

In Share my spot, enter your parking spot number, choose underground/above ground, set start/end date-time and submit.

3) How do I claim a spot quickly?

Use the Claim button directly in a free-spot row, or use the claim form with filters.

4) What if I claim only part of the period?

Your claimed interval is reserved, and the remaining time stays available for other neighbours.

5) What can admin do?

Admin can manage users, create shared spots, and monitor activity.
